Welcome to the Academy of Finance

Academy of Finance

Lead Teacher: Ms. Natasha Lopez

Introduction: In the Academy of Finance you will pick two tracks before 10th grade which is the finance and entrepreneur tracks. Each have their own set of classes you will take with each having an EOC at the end with a project for every class. In the summer after Junior year, you will be able to take a required paid internship to gain experience in the field of finance. There are also special days called dress for success that will require all academy of finance students to wear a formal business clothing. Ms Lopez is the lead teacher of the academy and is the one in charge of all of the big events for the academy like getting the interships, reminding us for dress for success day, and exclusive fieldtrips.

Requirements for the Academy/Manget Program:

  • GPA must be 2.0 or higher (2.5 GPA or higher to stay in academy)
  • No more than 10 unexcused absences or tardies for the previous academic year

NAF Track Certification:

Upon completion, students will be certified and gain these benefits:

  • Make the connection between their high school coursework and their futures
  • Reflect on their own learning in order to set goals for continuous improvement
  • Gain proficiency in taking online assessments, which are becoming more prevalent in higher education
  • Demonstrate the hard and soft skills they develop throughout their academy experience
  • Get on track to NAFTrack Certified Hiring, a commitment made by top companies to give special consideration to NAFTrack certified job applicants
